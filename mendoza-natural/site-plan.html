Discover Natural Mendoza

Website Plan

Site Name

Discover Natural Mendoza

This name reflects an ecological and tourism-focused initiative to explore natural reserves and outdoor activities in the province of Mendoza, Argentina.

Optional domain: discovermendoza.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide clear and engaging information about eco-tourism activities in Mendoza, such as hiking, bird watching, and visiting nature reserves. It also allows users to book excursions online and learn about eco-friendly practices to help preserve nature.

Scenarios

  • Which natural reserves can I visit in Mendoza and what activities does each one offer?
  • How can I make a reservation for an eco-tour through this website?

Color Scheme

  • #2E8B57 (Forest Green) – For titles, headings, and primary buttons
  • #F4F4F4 (Light Gray) – Main background of the site
  • #6B4226 (Earth Brown) – Decorative elements and footer
  • #FFFFFF (White) – Text on dark buttons and cards

Typography

  • 'Montserrat', sans-serif – For headings and navigation
  • 'Open Sans', sans-serif – For body text and paragraphs

These fonts were chosen for their readability and clean, nature-friendly aesthetic.
